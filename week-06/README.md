# Week 06: AI-Augmented UI Design

## 🎯 Objective
Today, you will use Artificial Intelligence to "level up" your Erasmus Brew website. You will use an LLM to define a brand, a UI generator to reimagine your layout, and an image generator to create professional assets.

---

## 🛠 Task 1: The Brand Persona (LLM)
Before designing, we need a professional "vibe." 
1.  Open an LLM (Google Gemini, ChatGPT, or Claude).
2.  **Prompt the AI** to create a brand persona for "Erasmus Brew." Ask for:
    - Personality/Tone (e.g., "Minimalist & Tech-focused" or "Vintage & Cozy").
    - A 3-color palette (with HEX codes).
    - Typography suggestions (Google Fonts).
    - A 2-sentence marketing pitch.
3.  **Save the results:** Create a file called `brand-persona.txt` in your `/week-06/` folder and paste the AI's response there.

---

## 🎨 Task 2: Re-imagining the UI (UI Generation)
Choose **one** page you have already created (Home, Menu, or Contact).
1.  Use a UI generator tool like **Uizard (Autodesigner)**, **Galileo AI**, or **Google Stitch**.
2.  **Generate:** Describe your "Brand Persona" from Task 1 and ask the AI to generate a **Mobile UI** for that specific page.
3.  **Screenshot:** Take a screenshot of the AI's design. This will be your reference.

---

## 🖼️ Task 3: Elevating Assets (Image Generation)
1.  Use an AI Image generator (DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, or Canva Magic Media).
2.  **Generate** a high-quality Hero Image or Gallery image that matches your brand persona.
3.  **Implement:** Upload this new image to your WordPress site to replace your old placeholder.

---

## 🏗️ Task 4: The Manual Rebuild
Now, go back to your WordPress editor and "copy" the AI's best design choices onto your live page.
1.  Adjust your **colors** and **spacing** to match the AI's mockup.
2.  Update your **headings and text** using the brand tone from Task 1.
3.  Take a screenshot of your finished, improved page.

---

## 🏁 Expected Results
Your `/week-06/` folder on GitHub should contain these four files:
```text
/week-06/
  ├── before.png          (Screenshot of your page BEFORE AI)
  ├── after.png           (Screenshot of your page AFTER AI)
  ├── brand-persona.txt   (The full brand guide generated in Task 1)
  └── prompt.txt          (See structure below)
```

Your prompt.txt must be organized as follows:
```text

```
