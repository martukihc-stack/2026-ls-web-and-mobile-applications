# Week 08: The Visibility Layer (Search & Social)

## 🎯 Objective
Today, you will build the "Bridges" to your website. You will ensure that when someone finds you on Google or shares your link in a group chat, your brand looks professional, trustworthy, and inviting.

> **⚠️ Pantheon Note:** Because our sites are in "Sandbox" mode, external bots (like Facebook or Google) cannot see them yet. We will use **In-Browser Simulators** to test our work today.

---

## 🛠 Task 1: The "Social Card" (Open Graph)
When you share a link on WhatsApp, you want a beautiful photo and a catchy description to appear, not just a plain URL.

1.  Open your **Home Page** in the WordPress editor.
2.  Open the **RankMath** sidebar and click the **Social** tab (looks like a share icon).
3.  **Set the Social Image:** Upload your high-quality AI-generated hero image.
4.  **Set the Title & Description:** Write a "Hook" (e.g., *"Prague's coziest study spot. ☕ Get your 2-for-1 Erasmus voucher inside!"*).
5.  **The Test:** Use the RankMath preview window to see how it looks.

---

## 🔍 Task 2: Keywords & Search Snippets
How will your site look on a Google Results page?

1.  In RankMath, go to **General** -> **Edit Snippet**.
2.  **Focus Keyword:** Set this to `Coffee Shop Prague` or `Erasmus Cafe`.
3.  **The Permalink:** Ensure your URL "slugs" are clean (e.g., `/contact` instead of `/?p=123`).
4.  **Title Tag:** Make sure it's under 60 characters so it doesn't get cut off.

---

## 🗺️ Task 3: The Sitemap (The Bot's Map)
Even if Google can't crawl us yet, we need to prepare the "Map."

1.  In your WordPress Dashboard, go to **RankMath** -> **Sitemap Settings**.
2.  Copy the URL of your sitemap (usually it ends in `/sitemap_index.xml`).
3.  **The Exploration:** Paste that URL into your browser. This is what a Search Engine "sees" when it wants to understand your site's structure.

---

## 🤝 Task 4: Social Proof & Links
A brand without social links feels like a "ghost town."

1.  Add a **Social Icons** block to your Footer.
2.  Link them to your (real or fictional) Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook pages.
3.  **Bonus:** Add a "Share this Menu" button to your Menu page so users can easily send your prices to their friends.
