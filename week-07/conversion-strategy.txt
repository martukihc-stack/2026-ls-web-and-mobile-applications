The fields selected in the WPForms form are:

- Name: Necessary to personalize future automated communications and build trust.
- Email: The primary channel for sending the Lead Magnet (PDF) and future loyalty campaigns.
- University (Dropdown): A strategic field to segment promotions by campus (e.g., Charles University, CTU, VSE) and optimize logistics for local events or pop-up stores.
