Brand Persona for Erasmus Brew / MCoFFee

Brand Name:
Erasmus Brew

Current Website Context:
The existing website presents MCoFFee as a local coffee shop dedicated to students, with a cozy environment for studying and a simple menu including coffee, tea, and cake. The Week 06 redesign keeps this student-focused concept but makes it more professional, warm, and international.

Personality / Tone:
Erasmus Brew has a warm, social, and international personality. The tone is friendly, welcoming, and slightly playful, designed for Erasmus students, travelers, and young coffee lovers who want a cozy place to meet, study, and relax. The brand feels modern but cozy, combining student life, coffee culture, and the feeling of discovering a new city.

Brand Style:
Vintage & Cozy with a modern student-friendly touch.

Color Palette:
1. Espresso Brown - #3B1F0A
   Used for headings, navigation, footer, and strong brand elements. This color keeps continuity with the original website style.

2. Cream Background - #F5F0E8
   Used as the main background color to create a soft, warm, café-inspired feeling. This also keeps continuity with the original website.

3. Erasmus Teal - #2A9D8F
   Used for buttons, links, highlights, cards, and interactive elements. It adds a fresh international/student feeling to the warm coffee palette.

Typography Suggestions:
1. Playfair Display
   Best for headings and hero titles. It gives the website a stylish and elegant café feeling.

2. Poppins
   Best for body text, buttons, menu items, and navigation. It is clean, modern, and easy to read on mobile.

3. Optional Alternative: Lora
   A good choice for subtitles or short descriptive sections if a softer editorial style is desired.

Marketing Pitch:
Erasmus Brew is a cozy coffee spot where students, travelers, and locals can meet, study, and share stories over great coffee. With a warm atmosphere, international energy, and student-friendly menu, Erasmus Brew turns every visit into a small cultural experience.
